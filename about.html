About Rasha Bilash

Rasha Bilash was founded by three friends who came from different parts of Bangladesh with a shared dream of owning a restaurant and serving their community in the USA. With a passion for authentic Bangladeshi cuisine, they created Rasha Bilash to bring the flavors of home to Brooklyn.
